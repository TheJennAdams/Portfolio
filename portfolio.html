UX/UI Design

I'm working through the Google Professional Certificate for UX/UI and following along with Figma tutorials so that I can build my own app: A reminder app that actually works! So many reminder apps have failed to be helpful in the ways I needed them to, that I'm taking things into my own hands and designing my own.


Take a peek at my mood board!

My main goal with this app is to get a widget on your homescreen that will change throughout the day to get your attention and remind you to do things. It could be anything from a reminder to drink some water or brush your teeth, to remembering to call the doctor, pick up milk on the way home, or call your grandma and tell her you love her!
