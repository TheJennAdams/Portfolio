    Purple Floral

    This is a hardbound book I created by sewing together signatures of paper, attaching pieces of book board to a piece of floral fabric, and gluing a thin purple ribbon into the spine.

    Special Touches

    I painted the edges of the book with a silver paint to give the finished product an extra special touch, as well as using alternating purple and gray thread on the head and tail bands.

    An Inside View

    A view of the inside cover, with finished end papers glued neatly over the case supports.

    Wood Cover

    I wanted this book to look like a garden gate. I cut pieces of wood to size, made vertical grooves down the front, stained it, and glued on some fun hardware that I thought made it look pretty legit.

    Secret Belgian Binding

    I bound this book using what is called The Secret Belgian Binding. I wanted to try something unique and different, and I really love how it turned out. You can learn more about this bookbinding method in Anne Goy's book, Crisscross binding

    Crisscross Binding

    A close up view of the Secret Belgian Binding, or Criss-cross binding" on this book.

    Paper Binding
