    Logos

    The Logo for this web page was created using medibang paint. I found a font I liked, but it didn't have a cross on the top of the J. I usually don't even cross my Js anyway but I didn't like how that looked in this font. So I took the letter I and rotated it and stuck it on top of the J and voilà! Then I took that same I and shortened it a bit and stuck it onto the A to make it more star-like. Below that is a logo I created for my Portfolio page.
